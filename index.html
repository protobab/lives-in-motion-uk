Legal

Regulatory Compliance

Ofcom Editorial Alignment

Lives In Motion operates in adherence to the Ofcom Broadcasting Code. Our mission is built on accuracy, impartiality, and the prevention of harm. All community-submitted content is vetted by our editorial team before broadcast.

Privacy & Data (GDPR)

Submissions via WhatsApp or Email grant Lives In Motion a non-exclusive license for broadcast use. Your personal contact data is never shared with third parties and is used solely for studio communication.

Equality & Diversity

We are a platform for all voices within the Nigerian Diaspora. We maintain a zero-tolerance policy for hate speech, discrimination, or content that incites community tension.
